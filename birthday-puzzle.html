A Note on How This Was Made

This birthday puzzle was built with the help of AI tools — specifically, Claude assisted me in writing the code that powers it.

I want to be transparent about that, and also about the care I put into the decisions that AI didn't make for me.

I thought carefully about our babies who would use this. No personal information about them is collected, stored, or transmitted anywhere, and any details about them (like their name and age) live only in that moment and in that link. I chose themes, difficulty levels, and a reward structure with their ages and attention spans in mind. The aesthetic was a deliberate choice away from the overstimulating, attention-engineered design patterns that dominate children's digital experiences. I made something that felt quieter, warmer, and more like a handmade gift from their Auntie, who loves them.

The love behind this is mine. I just needed help with the code.
